# FlashPad Privacy Policy

FlashPad does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

- **No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry.** FlashPad makes no network
  connections of its own.
- **Your files stay on your device.** FlashPad only reads and writes the files
  you explicitly open or save. It runs in the macOS App Sandbox and can only
  access files you choose.
- **Local settings only.** Preferences (such as your list of recently opened
  files and window size) are stored locally on your Mac and are never sent
  anywhere.

If you have questions, open an issue at
https://github.com/zeljan-alduk/flashpad.
